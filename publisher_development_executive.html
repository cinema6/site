Publisher Development Executive

CINEMA6 is looking for an experienced publisher solutions executive to lead the strategic growth of CINEMA6’s publisher business. This high-visibility role will lead the acquisition, retention, and development of CINEMA6’s publisher base with the goal of driving deployment of CINEMA6’s innovative video products across desktop, tablet, and mobile inventory.

Responsibilities

  • BUILD RELATIONSHIPS: Develop and manage relationships with key publisher accounts
  • LEAD ROLLOUTS: Lead publisher rollout of CINEMA6’s product including developing rollout strategy and publisher targets
  • RECRUIT: Player/coach leader of the publisher development team, including recruiting and developing the team and development of best practices
  • IMPLEMENT DISTRIBUTION: Evaluate and implement strategic distribution opportunities and product integrations
  • REPRESENT US: Represent CINEMA6 at leading online publisher conferences
  • IMPROVE PUBLISHERS: Understand the needs of online publishers and how CINEMA6 ad products improve their business
  • IDENTIFY OPPORTUNITIES: Develop publisher prospect lists including identifying key decision makers at target prospects
  • PITCH PUBLISHERS: Conduct product demos and presentations to demonstrate the CINEMA6 value proposition and close new publisher clients
  • MANAGE NEW FEATURES: Collect and communicate feedback from publishers to product development team in order to enhance the CINEMA6 product suite
  • FOSTER RELATIONSHIPS: Partner with account management team to ensure good post-deployment relationships with new clients

Requirements

  • • Minimum of five years in selling to and negotiating partnerships or distribution agreements with large publishers
  • • Strong track record of achieving aggressive goals and of managing high-performance team
  • • Self starter with ability to own negotiation process from initial meeting to close
  • • Condfident client presentation skills
  • • Close direct publisher relationships
